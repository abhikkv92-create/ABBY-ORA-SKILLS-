## 💡 Pro Tips for Research Success

To get the most precise research insights:

1. **Define Your Objective**: What decision are you trying to inform?
   - ❌ "What's new in Oracle?"
   - ✅ "Should we invest in AI for demand forecasting given our 2025 budget cycle?"

2. **Specify Time Horizon**: Are you looking for now, next quarter, or strategic planning?
   - "What can I implement in Q1 2025?"
   - "What should we be preparing for 2026-2027?"
   - "What's the 3-5 year strategic outlook for our industry?"

3. **Share Your Context**: What modules, industry, and pain points matter most?

   - "How practical is blockchain for our track & trace needs?"

5. **Request Practical Guidance**: What do you need to act on this information?
   - "What skills should we start developing now?"
   - "What pilot projects would you recommend?"
   - "How do we build a business case for this investment?"

6. **Ask for Follow-ups**: I remember context and can dive deeper
   - Start with overview, then drill into specific technologies, then implementation
